About

With our locations strategically placed throughout around San Diego, we bring our unique blend of traditional flavors and SoCal vibes to every corner of the city. Our passionate team is dedicated to infusing each taco with a distinct personality that reflects the spirit of our beloved city. We believe that great tacos shouldn’t break the bank. That’s why we invite you to join us every Tuesday for our legendary Taco Tuesday promotion. Indulge in our mouthwatering tacos while enjoying a fantastic deal of 10% off all food. And to complement your taco feast, we offer ice-cold Mexican beers for just $2.50. It’s the perfect way to satisfy your cravings and celebrate the flavors of Mexico without breaking the bank.

At City Tacos, we embrace culinary creativity by offering a diverse menu that caters to all tastes. Whether you’re a meat lover, a seafood aficionado, a vegetarian, or following a Paleo lifestyle, our menu has something special just for you. Each dish is thoughtfully crafted using the finest ingredients, resulting in mouthwatering tacos that are as visually stunning as they are delicious.
